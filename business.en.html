Smart HACCP​

What is Smart HACCP?

Smart HACCP is a comprehensive quality management system that manages Critical Control Point (CCP) monitoring data in the food manufacturing process using IoT technology.
It digitalizes technical logs and quality-related data, allowing for real-time automatic recording, management, verification, and storage.
This prevents data tampering and facilitates analysis and usage, extending to production management and inventory in/out management.

On-Site Automation and Data Collection
Field Terminals

Operato Chef, in collaboration with expert partners, provides equipment to support tasks required in the production field, such as data collection, work order receipt, and recipe verification.

Field terminals used in food and beverage manufacturing must be resilient to humidity, salinity, acid, and other factors, and must be washable with water, unlike other factory environments.
We meticulously check the selection of products and their suitability for the field.

Automation Equipment

To improve production efficiency, various automation equipment must be reviewed from the design/ordering stage in terms of the types and methods of data collection.

Operato Chef provides methods for data integration from the design/ordering stage, in collaboration with the customer-specified automation equipment suppliers or partners, to ensure the effectiveness of the Smart Factory.

Sensors

Sensors in the food and beverage manufacturing field do more than just collect process data; they must also ensure the reliability of HACCP-related data.

Operato Chef, with its extensive know-how in semiconductor distribution, assists in the crucial first step of eliminating paper from the field through the selection of the right sensors.
